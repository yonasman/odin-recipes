Shiro (the Ethiopian traditional food)

Shiro is Ethiopians tranditional food. The food is so delicious that it's loved by almost all Ethiopian citizens.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Paper
  • Shiro spice
  • water and salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the onion
  2. Add the paper and other spices
  3. Add salt and you're done
